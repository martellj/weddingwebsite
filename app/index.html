About Us

John and Casey's love story begins with two computers, a few half decent photographs and the hope of finding true love. Casey arrived at Black Sheep Coal Fired Pizza with butterflies in her stomach. She found it difficult to maneuver into the parallel parking spot with her sweaty palms. The nervousness was due to the circumstances of the date. Because Casey met John online she wasn't sure what to expect. As she struggled to fit her Kia in its proper place she noticed a handsome man staring at her parking attempt with a horrified look on his face.

John was not sure how someone could struggle so much to fit into a parking spot. Inside was a woman laughing self-deprecatingly; she had a smile that made his heart warm. Yes, this was his companion for the evening. John and Casey spent the next two hours talking and laughing over pizza. Sharing stories from their past and connecting over their dreams for the future. As they separated for the evening they were strangers no more. Neither could have imagined that night was the beginning of an extraordinary journey. A journey that would take the pair through good times and bad. In the end, John and Casey will forever be thankful their mouses clicked paths. John and Casey are humbled by your support and grateful that you will part of the next chapter of their journey.

John
